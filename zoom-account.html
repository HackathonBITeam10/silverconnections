How to Create a Zoom Account

To use Zoom, you will need to sign up for a free account.

Step 1:

Open your internet browser and search for zoom.us.

Step 2:

Click on the “Sign Up, It’s Free” button on the top right of the screen.

Step 3:

You will be asked to type your email address in the text box provided. Once you have done this, click the “Sign Up” button below.

Step 4:

Zoom will send you an email with a link to finish setting up your account. Click on the link and enter your name and then enter a password.

Step 5:

Once you have created your account, open the Zoom app on your computer, tablet or mobile phone and sign in with the email address and password you provided.
