How to enable FaceTime

FaceTime is standard on all Apple devices however if it doen't work this may because it has not been enabled. This guide will help you to enable FaceTime if required


Note: the orange boxes hide personal information and will show your information

Step 1:

Open Settings.

Step 2:

Click into the search bar and type Facetime.

Step 3:

Tap the FaceTime search result.

Step 4:

Press the slider to active FaceTime.

Step 5:

FaceTime is active when the slider is green.
