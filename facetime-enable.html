How to enable FaceTime

FaceTime is standard on all Apple devices however it may not have been enabled. This guide will help you to enable FaceTime if required

Open Settings.

Click into the search bar.

Search for FaceTime.

Press the slider to active FaceTime.

FaceTime is active when the slider is green.
