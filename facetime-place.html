Placing a Facetime Call

To place a FaceTime call you will need;

  1. An Apple Device (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, and Mac)
  2. An Internet Connections
  3. To have enabled FaceTime

Open FaceTime on your Apple Device.

Tap the + button.

Type the name, email address or number that you want to call.

Tap Audio or Video to start your call.
