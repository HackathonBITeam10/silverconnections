Answering a Facetime Call

To Answer a FaceTime call you will need;

  1. An Apple Device (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, and Mac)
  2. An Internet Connections
  3. To have someone calling you on FaceTime

Step 1:

When a call comes in, the caller’s name prominently displays on the screen.

Step 2a:

If your device is locked when a FaceTime call comes in, slide the green arrow button to the right to answer. To decline it, do nothing and wait for the caller to give up.

Step 2b:

If your device is unlocked tap Accept to answer the call or Decline if you’d rather not.
